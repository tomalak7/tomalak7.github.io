
Tomalak Oleksandr is Associate Professor at the Institute of Theoretical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He works on various subjects in modern Nuclear and Particle Physics, in a close connection to the experiment. 

Research Directions:
Neutrino physics, Nucleon structure, Radiative corrections, Effective field theory
